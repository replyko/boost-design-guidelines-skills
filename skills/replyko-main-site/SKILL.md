## Implementation Intent

- Optimize for product explanation, trust, and signup flow
- Use Coral only on real conversion actions
- Keep spacing generous and sections easy to scan
- Make feature and pricing areas feel structured and credible
- Follow existing Blade and localized route patterns instead of inventing a separate frontend structure

## Avoid

- Product-dashboard density
- Decorative Coral
- Off-brand fonts or neutral palettes
- Unclear hero messaging and weak primary CTA emphasis
